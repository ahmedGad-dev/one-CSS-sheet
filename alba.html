About The Project

Services

Maadi gardens compound contains all kinds of services, it falls in a very active area on the ring road near El-Kattamya shooting club, El-maadi club, Yackht club (the investements area) 1KM far away from carefour El-maadi and schools collection. Very close to modern academy and the modern british school

Location

Maadi Gardens epic location allows you to reach all of the most important areas around cairo and Giza in less than 30 minutes either direction where the first direction is (Maadi, Mohandseen, El-Haram, and 6th of october) and the other direction of (Heliopolis , The airport, Nasr City and 5th Settelement)

Security

Maadi Gardens was built on area of 20 acres, 84000 meters in order to build a full services Residence compound where all of the cozy life styles are available in addition to that it's supported by a group of different services including electronic gates equipped with security cameras also security guards on the gates and a guard for every building.
